Oily / Acne Prone

Oily skin occurs when glands in the skin make too much sebum, which is the waxy substance that seals in moisture. Oily skin generally has a thicker texture, dilated pores, and a dull, greasy and shiny appearance. This appearance is due to excess sebum, which promotes the appearance of skin blemishes such as “blackheads” and “pimples”.
