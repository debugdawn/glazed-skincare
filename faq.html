    FAQ

    Is our range effective + natural?

    Glaze skincare is formulated to give you clear and glowing skin. it is made by experts, using the best quality ingredients and is dermatologist approved. products use a combination of natural and scientific ingredients that focus on getting you results.

    Is Glazed local?

    We are proudly local and produced in South Africa

    Who can use Glaze products?

    Glaze is a diverse and inclusive skincare brand. Products work for all ages and is made for all skin types, tones and cultures.

    When will I start seeing results?

    Some results like hydration and glow can be seen almost immediately. With other concerns such as hyperpigmentation and acne, you may start seeing results within 4 weeks, but for some it might take a bit longer.

    What level of active ingredients does Glaze use?

    Most of our products contain multiple actives, so we have taken great care to formulate at levels and combinations that lead to the best results. Certain ingredients from suppliers are powerful. therefore we use at the maximum and optimal recommended levels that wont cause irritation.

    What is Glazed products' expiry date?

    Glazed products have a 2 year shelf life. Recommended period-after-opening (POA) is 6 months.We advises using the product within this timeframe to ensure the best quality and efficacy.
