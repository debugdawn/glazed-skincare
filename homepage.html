    what is “glazed donut” skin? “glazed donut” skincare is all about looking glossy, shiny, and hydrated. maintaining adequate hydration helps keep your skin barrier intact, which in turn helps your skin look dewier and younger. it is a Korean skincare trend that helps achieve glass-like poreless skin that is luminous and shiny without looking oily or greasy. this type of skin doesn't only appear youthful and smooth but also gives a healthy well-hydrated feel to the skin, almost like a glazed donut. our companies mission is to provide our custimers with healthy skin using ingredients that are 100% natureal and made for all skin types. we aim to make sincare that is clean, simple, effective and affordable.
